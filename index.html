Overview

This speedDial expansion changes standard browser tab to minimal UI and fast new tab page with your bookmarks.

The main advantage of Speed Dial in comparison with the standard page is that you can add the links to the websites of your choice so you always have direct access to them.

Also with ability of "import and export" functionality save bookmarks as .txt file to your PC. Your bookmarks will not be lost after browser or system reinstallation. This function also allows you to transfer your saved bookmarks to other computers with speedDial installed instead of standard page.

The expansion also adds links for two major search engines, use as prefered.
